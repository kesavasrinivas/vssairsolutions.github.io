What We Do

Comprehensive HVAC & Clean Room Services

From design to installation, validation and maintenance — we are your single-window solution for clean, controlled environments.

Air Handling Units

Double skin & single skin AHUs in capacities of 1,000 to 25,000 CFM. Ceiling, vertical & horizontal floor-mounted configurations for industrial and pharmaceutical use.

HEPA & AHU Filters

Pre-fine filters, bag filters, deep pleat & mini-pleat HEPA filters in microvee or rod-type construction with high efficiency and low resistance performance.

Cooling & Heating Coils

DX and chilled-water coils designed for best heat-exchange performance, tested to AHRI standard 410, with copper tubes and aluminium/copper fins.

Clean Room Solutions

Laminar Air Flow units, Air Showers, Pass Boxes, clean room partitions, airtight false ceilings, garment storage cabinets, doors and SS accessories.

Validation Services

Air changes per hour testing, velocity measurement, HEPA integrity (PAO) test, non-viable particle count, air flow visualisation, pressure balancing and T&RH mapping.

Annual Maintenance (AMC)

Comprehensive after-sales services including warranty, preventive maintenance, troubleshooting and system upgrades — keeping your facility compliant year-round.
