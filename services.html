Product

Air Handling Units (AHUs)

VSS Air Solutions supplies Air Handling Units in varied capacities and configurations for comfort (commercial) and process cooling (industrial) applications. Our AHUs serve power plants, packaging units, hospitals, pharmaceutical and processed food facilities.

Designed and fabricated for trouble-free operation for years to come, our AHUs are available in capacities from 1,000 CFM to 25,000 CFM.

Product

Filters — Pre-Fine to HEPA

Our range of AHU filters covers the full spectrum from coarse pre-filters to high-efficiency HEPA units. Pre-fine and fine filters are available in Box/Flange type in aluminium and MS powder-coated frames with washable or air-cleanable media (HDPE or synthetic non-woven).

Pocket filters feature ultrasonic-welded design (not stitched), ensuring zero leakage. Designed for large flow, high efficiency, low resistance and high dust-holding capacity.

Pre-Fine Filters (0.3µ – 20µ) Bag Filters HEPA (Deep Pleat) HEPA (Mini Pleat) Microvee Construction Rod Type

Cooling & Heating Coils

Our DX and chilled-water cooling/heating coils are designed and selected to deliver best-in-class heat exchange performance for any set of parameters. Manufactured from seamless copper tubes with aluminium/copper fins and tested to AHRI Standard 410.

Each coil is designed specifically for its media and application, maximising heat exchange. Stainless steel coils and SS fins are available as an option for demanding environments.

Clean Room Solutions

VSS Air Solutions specialises in the complete range of clean room services — from design and supply to installation and validation. We custom-build systems for pharmaceutical, research and industrial clean rooms.

Our clean room product line includes Laminar Air Flow units, Reverse LAF (dispensing booths), Air Showers, Pass Boxes, partitions, airtight false ceilings, and all associated SS accessories.

Laminar Air Flow (H/V/M) Reverse LAF (Dispensing Booth) Air Shower Pass Box (Static & Dynamic) Clean Room Partitions Airtight False Ceiling SS Furniture & Accessories Epoxy / ESD Flooring
Service

Validation & Qualification Services

Our team has extensive experience supporting pharmaceutical FDA audits through rigorous validation protocols. We perform the full suite of clean room qualification tests to ensure your facility meets regulatory standards.

  • Air Changes per Hour (ACH) measurement
  • Velocity measurement tests
  • HEPA Integrity Test (PAO / DOP)
  • Non-Viable Particle Count Test
  • Air Flow Visualisation Test (Smoke)
  • Pressure Balancing & Differential Pressure
  • Temperature & Relative Humidity (RH) Mapping
Service
