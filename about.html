About PURUS Air Solutions

Our Story

Built on Expertise, Driven by Excellence

PURUS Air Solutions Private Limited was established in 2019 in Hyderabad with a clear purpose — to become a comprehensive solution platform for HVAC systems that deliver better, cleaner environments for working and production areas.

Since our founding, we have been proficient in making ideal decisions for any HVAC requirement through expert guidance, custom-built frameworks, and dependable systems that meet the highest standards of clean room technology.

We are widely recognised for our responsiveness, craftsmanship, and quality of work. Our HVAC professionals have completed several assignments and left a large number of clients satisfied with our services.

  • Founded 2019 in Hyderabad, Telangana
  • Specialises in pharmaceutical clean room environments
  • Team experience includes FDA audit support
  • Custom-designed systems for every project
